Welcome to Six Second Scribbles!

Six second scribbles is a game you play with friends. Gather in the same room or on a call, and start scribbling!

Gameplay Basics

The goal of Six Second Scribbles is to draw things and earn points. The player with the most points at the end wins the game.

The game consists of rounds, and you can play as many rounds as you'd like. Each round you will be given a new card to draw, and a new opponent to guess for.

Round Structure

Each round is split into two phases:

  • The Drawing Phase: Players receive ten prompts and a category. You have 60 seconds to draw as many prompts as you can.
  • The Guessing Phase: Each player receives another player's drawing card, and has to guess as many prompts as possible. Both the guesser and the artist earn points for correct guesses.

The drawer will mark correct guesses by clicking on each prompt the guesser gets right. Let the points roll in!

After the guessing phase, see the leaderboard and vote on the difficulty for the next round.
